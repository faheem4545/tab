Balochistan

Balochistan is the largest province of Pakistan by area. It is also the least populated province, with an estimated population of 12.5 million as of 2017. It is bordered by the province of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa to the north, Punjab to the northeast, Sindh to the east, the Arabian Sea to the south, and Iran to the west. The provincial capital and largest city is Quetta.

Punjab

Punjab is Pakistan's second largest province by area after Balochistan and its most populous province, with an estimated population of over 110 million as of 2017. Forming the bulk of the transnational Punjab region, it is bordered by the Pakistani province of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa to the north, Azad Kashmir and Gilgit-Baltistan to the northeast, the Indian states of Jammu and Kashmir to the east, Rajasthan to the southeast, Sindh to the south, and the Arabian Sea to the west. The provincial capital is Lahore.

Sindh

Sindh is the third largest province of Pakistan by area, and the second largest province by population after Punjab. It is bordered by Balochistan to the west, Punjab to the north, and the Arabian Sea to the south. The provincial capital is Karachi, the largest city in Pakistan and the 7th largest city proper in the world.
